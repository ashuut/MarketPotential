# Dashboard Description

## Project Name: Agriculture Services Analysis

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of various agricultural services offered by our company. It offers valuable insights into service unit performance, market analysis, financials, and profitability for different services. The primary goal is to help us make data-driven decisions, optimize resources, and maximize profitability in our agricultural services business.

## Sections:

### 1. Service Unit and Service Potential

- **Objective**: Provide an overview of service units for each agricultural service.
- **Key Metrics**: Number of service units, potential growth based on historical data.
- **Importance**: Helps in understanding which services are in high demand and where we can expand.

### 2. Current Captured Market and Targeted Market

- **Objective**: Compare our current market share with the targeted market share.
- **Key Metrics**: Market share percentages.
- **Importance**: Identifies opportunities for market expansion and growth strategies.

### 3. Total Expenses and Total Turnover

- **Objective**: Analyze our overall financial health.
- **Key Metrics**: Total expenses, total turnover.
- **Importance**: Critical for managing profitability and financial stability.

### 4. Turnover by Expenses as per Services

- **Objective**: Break down turnover by expenses for each agricultural service.
- **Key Metrics**: Revenue, expenses, and profitability for each service.
- **Importance**: Helps in identifying cost-effective services and areas for optimization.

### 5. Net Profit/Net Loss by Services

- **Objective**: Analyze the profitability of each service.
- **Key Metrics**: Net profit or net loss for each service.
- **Importance**: Guides decision-making and resource allocation for service improvement.

## Getting Started

1. Clone or download this repository to your local machine.

2. Ensure you have [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/en-us/desktop/) installed.

3. Connect the dashboard to your own data sources. Replace sample data for accurate analysis.

4. Customize the dashboard as needed, adding metrics or modifying visualizations.

5. Interact with the dashboard by using filters and visualizations to gain actionable insights.
